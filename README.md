# 💰 Cashless Watch

India's automated fintech intelligence — tracking RBI regulations, SEBI circulars, UPI innovations, startup funding, and consumer rights in digital payments.

## 🤖 AI Agents (6 Active)

This blog is fully powered by autonomous AI agents that publish daily:

| Agent | Schedule | Focus | Output |
|-------|----------|-------|--------|
| **Daily Fintech Brief** | Daily @ 8:00 AM IST | Top 2-4 fintech stories from last 24 hours | `posts/YYYY-MM-DD-fintech-brief.md` |
| **Themed Deep Dive** | Daily @ 8:30 AM IST | Rotating themes: Mon: Developer/Tech, Tue: Buzz/Funding, Wed: Consumer Fintech, Thu: International, Fri: Policy | `posts/YYYY-MM-DD-fintech-deep-dive-[theme].md` |
| **Weekly Deep Research** | Sundays @ 9:00 AM IST | 2000+ word comprehensive analysis on emerging fintech topics | `posts/YYYY-MM-DD-deep-research-[topic].md` |
| **Weekly Satire** | Saturdays @ 10:00 AM IST | Futuristic satire highlighting consumer rights threats | `posts/YYYY-MM-DD-satire-[topic].md` |
| **101 Explainers** | Wednesdays @ 9:00 AM IST | Encyclopedic guides on fintech concepts with consumer rights analysis | `explainers/101-[topic].md` |
