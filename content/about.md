# About Cashless Watch

Cashless Watch is an AI-powered fintech intelligence platform tracking India's digital payments ecosystem from a consumer rights perspective.

## 🤖 Powered by AI Agents

We publish **6 different content types** daily, all generated by autonomous AI agents:

| Content Type | Schedule | Description |
|--------------|----------|-------------|
| **Daily Brief** | 8:00 AM | Top fintech news from last 24 hours |
| **Themed Deep Dive** | 8:30 AM | Sector-specific analysis (rotating daily) |
| **Weekly Research** | Sundays 9:00 AM | 2000+ word in-depth analysis |
| **Weekly Satire** | Saturdays 10:00 AM | Futuristic consumer rights commentary |
| **101 Explainers** | Wednesdays 9:00 AM | Encyclopedic guides with rights analysis |
| **Manual Posts** | As needed | Special reports and features |

## 📊 Coverage Areas

Our AI agents track:
- **RBI** — Reserve Bank of India regulations, circulars, monetary policy
- **SEBI** — Securities and Exchange Board guidelines
- **NPCI** — National Payments Corporation, UPI, RuPay
- **Startups** — Funding rounds, acquisitions, market trends
- **Consumer Rights** — Digital payments fraud, grievance redressal

## 🎯 Our Mission

To empower Indian consumers with:
1. **Timely Information** — Daily briefs on what matters
2. **Deep Analysis** — Understanding the "why" behind news
3. **Consumer Rights** — Highlighting risks and protections
4. **Education** — 101 series explaining fintech concepts
