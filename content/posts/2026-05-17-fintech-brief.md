# Fintech Brief — May 17, 2026

## Today's Top Stories

### Nectar Social Raises $30M Series A for AI Marketing Platform

AI-powered marketing operating system Nectar Social announced a $30 million Series A round led by Menlo Ventures and its Anthology Fund. The company uses autonomous AI agents to help brands manage social activity, moderation, creator workflows, competitive intelligence, and commerce conversations across multiple platforms. Other investors include Gwyneth Paltrow's Kinship Ventures, GV, and True Ventures. The funding will support expansion in applied AI, engineering, and go-to-market efforts. [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/16/marketing-operating-system-nectar-social-raises-30m-series-a-in-round-led-by-menlo/)

### Solex Energy Reports Strong Q4FY26 Growth

Solex Energy reported consolidated net profit of ₹580 million for Q4FY26, up from ₹143 million YoY, with revenue surging 248.1% to ₹8,855.3 million. Full-year FY26 revenue from operations reached ₹16,180.6 million, up 144.3% from FY25. The company signed a landmark ₹4,000 crore MoU with the Government of Gujarat to establish a 5 GW solar cell manufacturing plant and a 10 GW BESS facility. The results were discussed at a post-earnings conference call on May 18, 2026. [scanx.trade](https://scanx.trade/stock-market-news/companies/solex-energy-q4fy26-revenue-surges-247-1-yoy-crosses-1-600-crore-milestone-in-fy26/39849390)

### India Curbs Certain Silver Imports

The government has curbed imports of certain silver products with immediate effect, shifting them from the "free" to "restricted" category. The move aims to ease pressure on foreign exchange reserves amid higher oil prices. The notification was issued on May 16, 2026. [Mining.com](https://www.mining.com/web/india-curbs-certain-silver-imports-with-immediate-effect/)
