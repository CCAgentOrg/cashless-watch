# Fintech Brief — June 19, 2026

## Today's Top Stories

### UPI Hits Record 23.2 Billion Transactions in May; PhonePe-GPay Combined Share Dips Below 80%

The National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) reported that UPI processed a record **23.20 billion transactions** worth **₹29.90 lakh crore** in May 2026. Volumes were up 24% year-on-year from 18.67 billion, while values rose 19% from ₹25.14 lakh crore.

In a notable shift, the combined market share of PhonePe and Google Pay fell **below 80% for the first time** since NPCI began publishing app-level statistics. PhonePe handled ~10.73 billion transactions (~46% share) and Google Pay processed ~7.59 billion (~33%), together accounting for roughly 79% of total volume. Paytm retained its third-place position, while newer entrants like Navi, super.money, BHIM and WhatsApp Pay continue to gradually chip away at the duopoly.

### India Yet to Decide on UPI-Alipay+ Integration

India is not expected to take an immediate decision on linking UPI with Ant International's Alipay+ network, with authorities still evaluating operational and security aspects. Officials have held discussions with Alipay+ representatives in recent months as part of broader cross-border digital payment expansion efforts, but no decision has been reached.

Any integration would allow Indian travellers to pay via UPI at Alipay+-connected merchants internationally. Sources clarified that discussions on Apple Pay integration with India's digital payments ecosystem have not been held either. India continues to expand UPI internationally — France became the first European country to accept UPI — while maintaining caution on infrastructure security.

[Source: Business Today](https://www.businesstoday.in/technology/story/upi-users-may-have-to-wait-india-yet-to-decide-on-chinas-alipay-integration-537767-2026-06-18)

### PM Modi at VivaTech Paris: "AI Means All Inclusive" — UPI Now Accepted at Eiffel Tower

Prime Minister Narendra Modi addressed the 10th edition of VivaTech in Paris as India's AI country partner, declaring that "AI means All Inclusive" and positioning India as the world's largest talent pool for technology. He highlighted India's digital transformation, from Aadhaar to UPI, noting that half of all real-time digital transactions globally now happen in India.

Key takeaways for fintech:

- **UPI in France**: PM confirmed UPI can now be used at the Eiffel Tower and Paris airport, with rollout expected at Charles de Gaulle and Nice airports shortly
- **India-EU FTA**: Modi referenced the historic India-EU free trade agreement concluded in early 2026, expected to boost trade, investment, and technology exchange
- **India-France Innovation Roadmap 2030**: 13 key outcomes announced, including a joint AI working group and expanded UPI presence in France
- The bilateral trade target of **$32 billion within five years** was reaffirmed

### RBI Extends Keki Mistry's Tenure as HDFC Bank Interim Chairman Till September 18

The Reserve Bank of India approved a three-month extension of Keki Mistry's tenure as HDFC Bank's interim part-time chairman until September 18, 2026. Mistry, a veteran of the HDFC Group, was first appointed interim chairman on March 18 following the resignation of non-executive chairman Atanu Chakraborty. The extension will remain valid until a regular part-time chairman is appointed.
